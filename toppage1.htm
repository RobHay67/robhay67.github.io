Blue Swoop specialise in Project Accounting services, software and systems.

Since 2001, we have been delivering production accounting services to the Global Entertainment Industry (encompassing Film, Television, Theatre, Music, Events and the Arts).

Blue Swoop has built powerful Project Cost Reporting Software (Pluto) that integrates with MYOB and QuickBooks to automate cost reporting for any size project or production.

Our accounting solution works for all types of projects in any industry - television, feature films, documentaries, theatre shows, exhibitions and sporting events including other industry projects such as infrastructure, factory builds, office fit-outs, IT & software development, science, medical, exploration and mining.
