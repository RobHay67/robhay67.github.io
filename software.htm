Software, Systems & Procedures

Got a project to manage? Pluto provides visibility into your project's current costs, commitments, tracking performance to accurately and reliably determine final cost projections measured against budget.

Blue Swoop's software, systems and procedures have been developed to deliver a reliable, flexible, user friendly and time efficient accounting solution for productions.

We have built a specialised cost reporting application (Pluto) that plugs directly into commercial accounting software packages (QuickBooks and MYOB) to automate the management and generation of cost reports. 

If you don't use QuickBooks or MYOB, Pluto can be easily customised to work with your accounting system.  We have configured Pluto to work for clients with SAP and Sun systems.

By combining Quickbooks or MYOB as the accounting engine for productions, and Pluto as the cost reporting tool, this results in a powerful system for managing and reporting for any size production.

Quickbooks and MYOB are popular accountings software packages used by professional accountants.  Choosing not to reinvent the wheel, Blue Swoop has avoided all the problems associated with specialist software development from scratch.  This means, by utilising commercially available accounting packages, the client can rely on all the advantages available from a multi national accounting software provider, such as 24/7 support and timely upgrades for compliance changes. 

Pluto provides the specialised production cost reporting that the commercial accounting software is not able to generate.

Blue Swoop is a professional partner and authorised distributor of Quickbooks and MYOB accounting software and has established systems and procedures using Quickbooks and MYOB for production accounting.

Blue Swoop has developed a cost reporting and management application (Pluto) for automation of cost reporting using data extracted from Quickbooks or MYOB.
